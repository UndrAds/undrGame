## Game Overview

In Ragdoll Archers, you're a stickman equipped with a bow and arrow, fighting against a variety of opponents. The game stands out with its ragdoll physics, allowing for unpredictable and often humorous character movements. You can earn points to upgrade your abilities and ammunition, adding an element of strategy to the game.

You can choose to play on your own or with a friend. When playing solo, you'll face waves of AI opponents, each more challenging than the last. And in PvP mode, you'll test your skills against your friend in an intense head-to-head battle.

## Key Features

### Ragdoll Physics

The ragdoll physics make every encounter in the game unpredictable and exciting. You'll have to adjust your aim and timing based on your character's wobbly movements.

### Upgrade System

As you progress through the game, you'll earn points that you can use to improve your archer's abilities and arrow types. This adds a layer of depth to the gameplay and lets you customize your game strategy.

### PvP and PvE Modes

Whether you prefer to play against AI opponents or against your friends, Ragdoll Archers has got you covered. The game features both PvP and PvE modes, offering a variety of gameplay experiences.
