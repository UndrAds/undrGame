Looking for a game with a unique twist? Check out House Of Hazards. This fun online puzzle game has you navigating through a home filled with everyday items turned into dangerous obstacles. You'll need quick reflexes and sharp decision-making skills to survive this domestic jungle.

## Game Overview

In House Of Hazards, your goal is to complete a series of household tasks while avoiding everything from hot stoves to falling chandeliers. Each level is a new room in the house, filled with its own unique set of challenges. And just to shake things up, you'll be competing against your friends in local multiplayer mode, where they can create even more chaos for you!

## Key Features

### Exciting Puzzle Mechanics

This isn't your typical puzzle game. Here, everyday objects become deadly hazards. A toaster, a television, even a staircase can be a potential hazard. You'll need quick wits and sharp reflexes to make it out alive.

### Local Multiplayer Mode

Compete against your friends to see who can complete the most tasks before time runs out. But watch out! Your friends can also trip you up by triggering hazards. It's a game of survival and strategy!

### Engaging Level Design

Each room in the house presents a new set of challenges. From the kitchen to the attic, you'll encounter a wide variety of hazards to avoid and tasks to complete.

### Simple Controls

House Of Hazards uses easy-to-learn controls, making it accessible for players of all levels. You'll be able to jump right into the action without having to memorize complex button combinations.

## Gameplay Details

Controlling your character in House Of Hazards is straightforward:

| Action | Key |
|--------|-----|
| Move | Arrow Keys |
| Jump | Space |
| Interact | E |

Use the arrow keys to move around and the Space bar to jump over obstacles. Press E to interact with objects and complete tasks. Remember, everything is a potential hazard, so stay sharp!
