HillClimb Racer is an addictive online racing game that keeps you on the edge of your seat. You'll take control of a plucky little car and tackle a variety of challenging, hilly terrains.

## Game Overview

In HillClimb Racer, your objective is to drive as far as possible while keeping your car from flipping over and running out of gas. Sounds simple, right? Well, the challenging physics and unpredictable terrain can make this a tricky feat to accomplish.

You'll be racing against the clock, trying to maximize your distances and collect coins to upgrade your ride. With each new run, you'll be pushing for that little bit extra, aiming to beat your previous record.

## Key Features

### Engaging Physics-Based Gameplay

HillClimb Racer offers a unique driving experience with its physics-based mechanics. The game's physics engine makes every hill climb and descent feel different, ensuring that no two runs are the same.

### Diverse Terrains

From steep hills to tricky bumps, the terrains in HillClimb Racer are designed to test your driving skills. Each terrain presents its own set of challenges, keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting.

### Vehicle Upgrades

As you race and collect coins, you'll get the chance to upgrade your vehicle. Improvements in speed, suspension, tires, and fuel efficiency can help you conquer the more challenging landscapes.

## Gameplay Details

The controls in HillClimb Racer are easy to learn but challenging to master:

| Action | Key |
|--------|-----|
| Accelerate | Up Arrow |
| Brake/Reverse | Down Arrow |
| Lean Forward | Right Arrow |
| Lean Backward | Left Arrow |

To succeed in the game, you'll need to balance speed and control, knowing when to push your vehicle to its limits and when to hold back and play it safe.
