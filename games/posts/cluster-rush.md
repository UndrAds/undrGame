## Game Overview

Cluster Rush is all about speed, reflexes, and quick decision-making. As the name suggests, you're in the midst of an ever-accelerating throng, and your primary goal is to avoid colliding with obstacles while also outpacing your cluster companions. With every second that ticks by, the challenge amps up, making this a game of endurance as much as speed.

## Key Features

### Engaging Gameplay

The gameplay in Cluster Rush is straightforward yet addictive. As you navigate through the obstacles, the exhilaration of the chase grips you, making each playthrough an intense experience.

### Competitive Scoring

In Cluster Rush, every step you take and every obstacle you dodge adds up to your final score. Push yourself to break your previous records and compete with friends for the top spot on the leaderboard.

### Easy Controls

Cluster Rush uses simple keyboard controls, making it easy to pick up and play. This makes the game accessible to players of all ages and skill levels.
