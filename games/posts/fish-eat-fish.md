## Game Overview

Fish Eat Fish is an online skill game where you navigate the depths of the ocean as a tiny fish. Your mission? Eat smaller fish to grow bigger while evading the larger fish that want to have you for dinner.

The game showcases a beautiful and vibrant underwater environment. The graphics are simple yet charming, filled with different types of fish, each with their own unique behaviors.

## Key Features

### Progressive Gameplay

Start as a small fish and progress your way to the top of the food chain. With each fish you consume, your own fish grows bigger and stronger, allowing you to take on tougher opponents.

### Challenging Opponents

The ocean is teeming with fish of all sizes. Some are small and easy to catch, while others are bigger and might give you a tough time. You'll need to use your wits to avoid the larger ones while hunting the smaller ones.

### Simple Controls

The game offers easy-to-learn controls. With just a few keystrokes, you'll be swimming, eating, and growing in no time.

## Gameplay Details

The controls for Fish Eat Fish are pretty straightforward:

| Action | Key |
|--------|-----|
| Move Up | Up Arrow |

Use the arrow keys to navigate your fish through the ocean. Eat smaller fish to grow bigger and avoid the larger ones. The bigger you grow, the more types of fish you can eat.
