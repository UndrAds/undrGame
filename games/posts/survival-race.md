## Game Overview

In Survival Race, your aim is to control a super-fast car and navigate through a perilous track filled with obstacles. The longer you survive, the higher your score. With every second of survival, the game's speed intensifies, making it a real challenge for your reflexes.

The game features a minimalist design that keeps you focused on the race. The controls are simple and intuitive, making it easy for anyone to pick up and play. But don't let its simplicity fool you; the game is packed with hours of high-speed fun.

## Key Features

### Endless Gameplay

Survival Race offers endless gameplay. The track is randomly generated each time you play, which keeps the game fresh and unpredictable.

### Increasing Difficulty

As time progresses, the game becomes more challenging. The speed of the car and frequency of obstacles increase, pushing your reflexes to the limit.

### High Score Leaderboard

The game features a leaderboard where you can compare your scores with players worldwide. Can you make it to the top?

### Simple Controls

Survival Race uses easy-to-learn controls. There are only two buttons to worry about, but mastering them is the real challenge.
